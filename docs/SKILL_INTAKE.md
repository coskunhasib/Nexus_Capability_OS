# Skill Intake

External skill references are not copied directly.

Nexus process:

1. Discover the skill reference.
2. Record claimed behavior.
3. Inspect available repo, docs, examples and usage notes.
4. Compare with existing Nexus skills, profiles, gates and packs.
5. Review current research and engineering references.
6. Identify gaps and improvement opportunities.
7. Rebuild the idea as a Nexus-native canonical skill.
8. Map it to related profiles, gates, tools and compatible packs.
9. Add evaluation hooks.
10. Validate references through registry audit and CI.

Status rule:

- draft: captured, not fully validated
- experimental: usable in trials
- active: validated across repeated trials
- deprecated: replaced or no longer useful
